This thorough investigation deserves attention and takes time.

When was the last time your doctor spent more than 20 minutes learning about you?

Functional labs tailored to find your root cause

  • 2 people with the same diagnosis can have very different underlying causes.
  • After contextualizing your struggles, I’ll select labs to help discover your contributing factors.

Aim of sustainable benefits

  • Pharmaceuticals can be essential for debilitating health conditions.
  • But suppressive drugs don’t rectify your underlying cause (and thus shouldn’t be your only treatment long-term).
  • Also, some drugs’ unwanted side effects and risk of dependency don’t make them ideal for sustainable options.

Customized treatment plans (specifically for you)

NDs are extensively trained in integrative medicine where they learn evidence-based treatments across many modalities.

Think of it like a toolbox where pharmaceuticals are just one option. Our job is to match your specific needs to the most appropriate tool in our toolbox.

You have unique priorities, abilities, genetics, biology, and psychology. I aim to create treatment plans that are practical for your lifestyle, sustainable, and ultimately align with your goals.

Treatments that evolve with your health journey

  • You have different needs at different stages of your journey towards optimal health.
  • 1-size-fits-all pill just doesn't make sense (for everyone with the same diagnosis or for every unique stage within someone’s evolution).

Options

  • I'll distill the overwhelming medical world to present pertinent options.
  • I'll explain the benefits and risks of each option (so you can make the best decisions for yourself).
