My Story

Mental health survivor becomes doctor

I’ve personally struggled through each of these symptoms and was desperate for relief.

I understand feeling hopeless, afraid, overwhelmed, shamed, not believed, desperate, exhausted, and wanting to give up.

Looking for answers

When seeking help, I was either given pills (that were my “best option”) with side effects I was not warned about or disregarded (since my body didn’t respond to treatments as expected).

But I didn’t get lasting relief.

I exhausted medical specialists around the world. By the 14th doctor consulted, I found a Naturopathic Doctor who assured me that my experiences were not fabricated and explored my root causes. The ripple effect
